About Us

A new Notes application in which you will have the opportunity to create notes, edit them, share them with friends. The program was created in 2023. In this web application, you need to register a new account, import your personal data that is protected (first name, last name, email, username, password), after which you can take notes, edit and share your notes with other people. Other people can go to your page and read the Notes that you dropped, just like you can read different notes, get information and get information about the news
